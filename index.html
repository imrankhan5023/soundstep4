The Reality

You want to help. But the
system makes it hard.

Thousands of families across the UK are waiting for support that isn't coming fast enough. And in the meantime, parents are left without the tools to help at home.

Long wait times

NHS speech and language waiting lists can stretch over 12 months, leaving families without professional guidance during critical developmental windows.

68,000+ children currently waiting

No guidance at home

Most parents want to help but don't know how to practise speech sounds correctly — and fear doing the wrong thing could slow their child's progress.

No structured home tools exist

No structured tools

There are almost no apps designed specifically for parents to support speech development at home — in a way that's safe, structured, and child-friendly.

Gap in the market
1 in 5 children have speech, language or communication needs
18+ months average NHS waiting time for speech therapy in some areas
74% of parents feel unsupported to help with speech at home
Our Approach

A calmer way to support
speech at home

SoundSteps is built around the idea that small, consistent practice matters more than occasional specialist sessions. We guide parents step by step — so you can practise the right sounds, at the right pace, with confidence.

  • Guided by speech-language research — every session follows proven practice principles
  • Built for everyday family life — short, calm sessions that fit around busy routines
  • No clinical background needed — designed for parents, not therapists
  • Complements NHS therapy — fills the gap between appointments, never replaces professional care

Our Mission

Why we're building
SoundSteps

Too many families are falling through the gaps. Children with speech needs are waiting too long, and parents are left without the tools to help. SoundSteps is being built by a small team who believe parents are capable of making a real difference — with the right support. Early Access families will directly shape the features, content, and direction of the app.

"Every child deserves a voice. Every parent deserves the tools to help them find it."

— The SoundSteps Team
🌱

Early matters most

Every child deserves support during the critical early years — not just those who reach the front of a waiting list in time. Early language skills are among the strongest predictors of academic success.

🏠

Home is powerful

Parent-led practice, done consistently at home, makes a measurable difference. SoundSteps makes that practice easy, guided, and genuinely effective for real families.

🤝

Built with parents

Early Access families shape everything — from features to language to how the app feels. This is a product built with parents, not just for them. Your voice matters.

SoundSteps

Supporting families to help their children find their voice — one step at a time.
